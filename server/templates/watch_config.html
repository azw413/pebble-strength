Strength — Watch Settings

Link this watch to your account by pasting a device token.

Get a token
  1. In your phone's browser (Safari / Chrome), open pebblestrength.app/devices and sign in with Google.
  2. Tap Create device token, then Copy.
  3. Return to this screen and paste it below.

This settings window is embedded and can't sign in to Google — use your normal browser to create the token, then come back here to paste it.
