Link this watch to your account by pasting a device token.

Get a token
  1. Open pebblestrength.app/devices ↗ in your phone's browser and sign in with Google.
  2. Tap Create device token, then Copy.
  3. Come back here and paste it below.

If that link opens inside this window instead of your browser, open pebblestrength.app/devices in Safari or Chrome yourself — Google sign-in doesn't work in this embedded window.
