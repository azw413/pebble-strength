What you can do
Guided workouts on the watch

Per-set rep targets, timed holds with a 3-2-1 lead-in, and skippable rest countdowns with vibration cues — fully usable mid-set with no phone in reach.

Automatic rep counting (coming soon…)

The accelerometer counts your reps as you go; a quick Up/Down on the watch corrects the count before it's logged.

Two-way sync

Build and edit workouts on the web and they appear on your wrist; the sets you perform sync straight back to your account.

Progress you can see

A dashboard of training volume, estimated 1-rep-max per exercise, bodyweight over time, and a full, editable session history.

Get started
  1. Sign in with Google above to create your account.
  2. Install the Strength watchapp on your Pebble — from the Rebble appstore or the Pebble appstore.
  3. Under Devices, create a device token, then paste it into the Strength app's settings gear in the Pebble phone app.
  4. Build workouts and assign them to your watch slots — they download to your wrist, and every set you do syncs back.
